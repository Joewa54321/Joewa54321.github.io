We do not collect any personal information. Our games do not require you to create an account or sign up in any way.

The only data stored on your device is related to game settings and preferences. This may include:

This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

4. Use of Collected Data

The locally stored data is used solely to enhance your gaming experience by:

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose any information collected from you to third parties.

We take reasonable measures to protect the data stored on your device. However, please be aware that no security system is impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

7. Children's Privacy

Our games are not directed towards children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.
